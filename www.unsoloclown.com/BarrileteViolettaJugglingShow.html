He is an elegant clown, an unpredictable illusionist, a disheveled juggler.

About

We met on December 2013 in a Festival of Street Art in Montevideo, Uruguay, in which we were both performing.

We started to work together as Un Solo Clown Company: we did several differents comic sketch and we performed in the street and in private and public events.

We also integrate the companies of various traditional circus in South America and Mexico, Emanuel like clown, magician and juggler and Violetta like aerialist acrobat.

On Summer 2018 we debut Barrilete Violetta Juggling Show: our dream to do an entire show about juggling, our passion, become true!

Emanuel

During a travel in Brasil he start to juggle with Devil's Stick and fire.

One day a man saw him juggling and told him: "My friend, that's not for you, you don't have grace or XXXX"

But he decided to train hard and get everyday a little bit better. Some months after, he performed his very first act in the Brasilian Juggling Convention, in front of an audience of 500 jugglers: it was a success.

Violetta

She start juggling by herself at the age of 13.

But she was so scared about performing in public that when someone look at her juggling, all the object were falling down all the time! She decided to perform like aerialist, and left the juggling behind.

But after their son born, she had to find different way to train abdominal muscle: she started to do hula hoop.

It was a revelation: she find her way to juggle and she was not scared anymore about the audience!
